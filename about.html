UR3 Robot Arm Manipulator

The UR3 robot arm, manufactured by Universal Robots, is a collaborative robotic arm known for its compact size and versatility.

Key features of the UR3 robot arm include:

  • 6 Degrees of Freedom (DOF): The UR3 robot arm has six joints, providing it with six degrees of freedom for flexible and precise movement.
    - Base rotation
    - Shoulder rotation
    - Elbow rotation
    - Wrist 1 rotation
    - Wrist 2 rotation
    - Wrist 3 rotation

  • Compact Design: With a payload capacity of 3 kg and a reach radius of 500 mm, the UR3 is designed to work in tight spaces and collaborate safely with human workers.

  • Collaborative Operation: The UR3 is designed to work safely alongside human operators without the need for safety cages, thanks to built-in force sensing and safety features.

  • Easy Programming: The UR3 robot arm can be programmed quickly and easily using intuitive graphical interfaces or scripting languages, allowing for rapid deployment in various applications.

  • Wide Range of Applications: The UR3 is suitable for a wide range of applications, including assembly, pick and place, packaging, machine tending, and more.

For more information about the UR3 robot arm manipulator, you can visit the Universal Robots website.
